## Long Description
Noor is your calm Muslim companion for daily worship, consistency, and trusted guidance.

Built for real Muslim life in Pakistan and beyond: accurate prayer times, Qibla direction, Quran reading, and practical step-by-step guides for Hajj, Umrah, and Janaza.

### Core Features
- Accurate prayer times with regional methods
- Sunni/Shia-aware setup (including Jafari method)
- Qibla direction with live compass
- Prayer tracker and weekly consistency stats
- Quran access with translation options
- Hajj guide (full day-by-day flow)
- Umrah guide (step-by-step with duas)
- Janaza guide (clear, structured emergency help)
- Daily goals, streaks, and motivation
- English + Urdu support

### Designed for Trust
- Clear references and educational disclaimers
- Calm, respectful UI for sacred use-cases
- Local-first experience and practical language

### Privacy
- Your data stays on your device
- No invasive tracking

Noor is an educational aid, not a replacement for qualified scholars. For religious rulings, consult your local scholar.
