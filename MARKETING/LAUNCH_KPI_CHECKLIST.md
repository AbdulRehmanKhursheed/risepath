# 30-Day Launch KPI Checklist

Use this every week. Decisions should be data-driven.

## Week 1 Targets
- Play Store listing conversion >= 12%
- Crash-free sessions >= 99%
- D1 retention >= 25%
- At least 20 written reviews

## Week 2 Targets
- D1 retention >= 30%
- D7 retention >= 10%
- Avg sessions/user/week >= 4
- Prayer tracker completion trend increasing

## Week 3 Targets
- D7 retention >= 12%
- Ad ARPDAU trend stable or improving
- Janaza/Hajj/Umrah guide opens increasing
- Negative review ratio < 15%

## Week 4 Targets
- D1 retention >= 32%
- D7 retention >= 14%
- Listing conversion >= 15%
- Organic installs overtaking paid installs

## Red Flags (Immediate Action)
- D1 < 20% for 7+ days
- Crash-free < 98.5%
- Ad complaints in reviews
- Users confused about prayer method or fiqh setup

## Weekly Review Format
- What improved?
- What dropped?
- Which feature drives return visits?
- Which video/ad creative drove best-quality installs?
- One decision for next week (keep / kill / improve)

## Growth Experiments Backlog
- Referral prompt after 7-day streak
- Gentle in-app review request after 5 completed prayers/day
- Urdu-first screenshot A/B test
- Ramadan mode teaser page
