# Privacy Policy — RisePath

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Overview

RisePath ("the App") is an Islamic lifestyle tracker developed to help Muslim users maintain their daily prayers, read Quran, and track spiritual habits. We take your privacy seriously.

---

## Data We Collect

### Data stored only on your device (local)
RisePath stores all personal data **locally on your device only** using AsyncStorage. We never upload, sell, or share this data. This includes:

- **Prayer records** — which of the 5 daily prayers you marked as prayed or missed
- **Mood entries** — your mood ratings and the motivational responses you received
- **Streak & goals** — your daily habit streak count and goal completion status
- **Prayer settings** — your selected calculation method and madhab
- **Location coordinates** — cached latitude/longitude to calculate accurate prayer times (stored locally, never sent to our servers)

### Location access
We request access to your device location **only** to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area. Location data is:
- Used only in real-time on your device
- Cached locally to avoid repeated permission requests
- Never transmitted to any server

### Quran content
Quran text is fetched from the public, open-source API at [alquran.cloud](https://alquran.cloud) and cached locally on your device. No personal data is sent in these requests.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect your name, email, phone number, or any account information
- We do **not** use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs
- We do **not** display advertising or share data with advertisers
- We do **not** have servers that store your data
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites

---

## Advertising

RisePath displays banner advertisements on the Home screen and Stats screen using **Google AdMob**.

- Ads are configured with the strictest **G-rated content filter** — adult, dating, alcohol, gambling and other inappropriate categories are blocked at the SDK level.
- We use keywords (`islamic, muslim, prayer, family, education`) to encourage contextually appropriate ads.
- Sacred screens — Quran reader, Prayer Tracker, Learn (duas & kalimas), Qibla direction, and the Umrah Guide — contain **zero advertisements** by design.
- Google may collect advertising identifiers (GAID/IDFA) and usage data to serve personalised ads. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.
- To opt out of personalised ads, users can reset their advertising ID in their device settings.

## Notifications

The App uses local device notifications to remind you of prayer times (5 minutes before each prayer). These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No data leaves your device to enable these reminders.

## Children

This App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Islamic Content Disclaimer

The Quranic ayahs displayed in this app are sourced from the `quran-uthmani` (Arabic), `en.sahih` (English — Saheeh International), and `ur.maududi` (Urdu — Maududi) editions via the alquran.cloud API.

Hadith references, dua texts, and scholarly benefit statements have been compiled with care. However:

- Some hadith referenced may be of varying authenticity grades (sahih, hasan, or da'if/weak).
- Where a narration is known to be debated or weak, a note is included.
- **Users are strongly advised to consult a qualified Islamic scholar** for personal religious rulings and to verify any hadith they wish to act upon.
- The App is intended as an educational and motivational aid, not a substitute for scholarly guidance.
